When running long tests on macOS, the machine may go to sleep if you don’t touch it.
There’s a built-in command that keeps it awake. 

caffeinate

Or run it with a command:

caffeinate -i ./run_test.sh

Let your Mac drink some coffee ☕️
Interesting and useful.
